Our Chef

Introducing our new Executive Chef, Adam Stevenson! Chef Adam brings over 30 years of culinary and hospitality experience to Copperleaf Restaurant and Cedarbrook Lodge. He began his career in Seattle at Tulio at the Hotel Vintage and Earth & Ocean at W Seattle. Most recently, he served as the Executive Chef at Motif Hotel and the Pro Sports Club in Bellevue. With a passion for sustainability and local ingredients (he already has exciting plans to enhance our Chef's Garden), Chef Adam will carry on Copperleaf's proud tradition of offering truly Northwest-centric cuisine on the Seattle Southside, and we can't wait for you to experience his creations for yourself. Please join us in welcoming Chef Adam!
